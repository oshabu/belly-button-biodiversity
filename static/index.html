Welcome to the Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard! hi readers! you will see a drop down menu to the left where you can select your subject ID. The info matching your metadata is displayed below in the Demographic Info panel. The top 10 bacteria cultures found in your belly button samples are shown in the chart to the right. The OTU numbers on the vertical axis refer to the Operational Taxonomic Unit (OTU) which groups bacteria with similar qualities. If you hover over the bars you can see names that belong to that OTU.
